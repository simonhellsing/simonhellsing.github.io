The challenge

Santander is one of Europes largest banks within the retail and corporate space. As part of a wider engagement of digital transformation with Brilliant Basics, I was part of a team that helped them envision a new way of making international payments for their corporate customers.


After having gotten a clearer picture of the users and their needs, we started ideating on what a future experience of making international payments could look like.

Mapping the as-is

As a Design Lead on the project I helped lead the work on establishing a direction of the UI that could challenge the current frameworks that had been developed for older Santander corporate services.
